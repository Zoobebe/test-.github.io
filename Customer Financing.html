BENEFITS OF WORKING WITH CANON

Flexible Terms and Options

We offer many different terms that provide you maximum flexibility. Standard term lengths from 12-months to 63-months are available and sometimes longer depending upon the asset type. For customers interested in owning the equipment at the end of the term, we have purchase options to satisfy that requirement.

Technology Refresh

Using financing can help businesses get the latest technology and equipment without making large upfront investments. This gives you the advantage of staying competitive and improving productivity.

Simplify Your Budget

Financing provides fixed payments over a specific period of time so you know exactly how much you need to budget each month.
